About CalioHealth

Built by people who know healthcare is broken — and have a plan to fix it.

CalioHealth is a Management Services Organization (MSO) built for the behavioral and mental health space. We don't replace clinicians — we give them superpowers. We don't just connect patients — we clear every barrier between them and help.

Our infrastructure layer sits between clinical care and operational complexity, absorbing the friction so providers can practice at the top of their license, patients can access care without obstacles, and payers can see the outcomes their dollars are producing.

We believe mental healthcare is one of the highest-leverage interventions in all of medicine. It deserves world-class operational infrastructure — and that's exactly what we're building.
